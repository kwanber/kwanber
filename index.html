Our Future Generation

  • Education in North cannot be over-emphasized as it is undoubtedly the most important factor to consider if Nortern Uganda has to develop and advance as a continent. However, most children in some parts of North still do not have access to education at all.
  • The word education is drawn from the Latin word educe which means that is to draw out from within, to develop and to make what is already gotten better. It can as well be defined as a learning process or experience which is for the betterment of an individual or society.
  • The Commonwealth Requires the Education of the People as the Safeguard of Order and Liberty
